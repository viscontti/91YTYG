91YTYG — The Iron Mascot

91YTYG is a meme community built around our beloved mascot — the Iron (Утюг).

What started as an inside joke grew into a full community with original art, IRL events, a sticker pack, and a life of its own. 21 unique variations, each with its own personality. Below you can meet all of them, check drop rates, and jump straight to any sticker. Check out our travels on the Iron Around the World page, or visit our Telegram channel.
