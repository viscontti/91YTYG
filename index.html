91YTYG — The Iron Mascot

91YTYG is a Ukrainian meme community built around our beloved mascot — the Iron (Утюг). What started as an inside joke grew into a full community with art, events, stickers, and a life of its own.

Our mascot exists as a full sticker pack on Telegram. Each variation has its own personality, style, and story. Scroll down to meet all 20 variations.
