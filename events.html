Our Events

The 91YTYG community doesn't just exist online. We get together IRL — at clubs, on campus, in dorms. Here's a record of where we've been.

Freshmen Welcome Night 2024

What: Посвята першокурсників — Freshmen Initiation Night

When: 2024

The annual freshmen welcome event. New students arrived, the community showed up, and the Iron was there. A night of introductions and first impressions.

Faculty Day 2025 — Club Forsazh

What: День факультету — Faculty Day

When: 2025

Where: Club Forsazh, Kyiv

Faculty Day at Club Forsazh — one of Kyiv's well-known venues. Music, people, energy. Easily one of the most memorable 91YTYG events.

Dynamo Event

Where: Kyiv

The Iron showed up to support. An unforgettable outing with the community.

Polyana Fridays

What: Weekly outdoor meetups

When: Every Friday

Where: Polyana — the green square on KPI campus

Every Friday when the weather allows, the community gathers at the Polyana. No agenda, no dress code — just good people and the Iron watching over.

Dormitory 8, KPI

Where: KPI Dormitory 8, Igor Sikorsky KPI, Kyiv

The home base. Dormitory 8 on KPI campus is where many community members live and where countless ideas, memes, and Iron variations were born.
